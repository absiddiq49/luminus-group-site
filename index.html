Our Identity

Introducing Luminus Group BD

"Providing Safe Food at a Reasonable Price, Moving Forward with Luminus!"

Welcome to Luminus Group BD, an IT-based export-oriented food and beverage organization. With over two decades of experience, our directors have been passionately involved in supplying essential goods nationwide. We aim to showcase the excellence of our homeland on the global stage. Inshallah.

Leading with Innovation

Luminus is at the forefront of transformation, managing all activities through cutting-edge software solutions. This seamless integration of people and technology forms the bedrock of our success.

Our Mission

To solve the global challenge of providing safe food. We are dedicated to making sure everyone can have access to safe, high-quality products at fair prices without compromising health or sustainability.

Our Vision

To become a leading global provider where technology transforms the food industry, making it more open, efficient, and ethical for a healthier future worldwide.
